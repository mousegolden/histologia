My goal has been to develop a virtual microscopy laboratory using the resources available to us. Dr. Sorenson and I have collaborated for many years to develop a better learning experience for students in histology courses. Histology Guide represents the best of these ideas.

My experience in microscopy, digital imaging, software development, and web programming were essential in developing this web site.

Download the sample chapters, Chapter 1 - Introduction and Chapter 14 - Gastrointestinal Tract, from the "Atlas of Human Histology: A Guide to Microscopic Structure of Cells, Tissues and Organs" by Robert L. Sorenson and T. Clark Brelje. The printed atlas is available from the University of Minnesota Book Store and is the atlas for use with the Virtual Histology Collection of Slides.
