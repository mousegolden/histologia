Jean E. Magney

1931 - 2010

Jean E. Magney drew many of the illustrations on this web site. She was also an excellent teacher and managed histology laboratories for many decades.

Illustrations

Many of the electron micrographs were collected for publication in the Color Atlas of Histology by Stanley L. Erlandsen and Jean E. Magney (Moseby-Year Book, Inc., 1992).
